Our Engagement Process

1

Research & Plan

Initial 45 – 90 minutes discussion of what we want to achieve. This helps us both get a feel of each other, define high level goals for the engagement, and get a sense of whether we are right for one another or not. If we're happy with one another, we proceed by signing a contract/NDA.

2

Business System Analysis

This is a variable length process. The end product is a set of use cases and wireframes, which you sign off on to produce initial guidance and direction for the development of an MVP or prototype. "Innovation is not about saying yes to everything. It's about saying NO to all but the most crucial features. "- Steve Jobs

3

Iterative Development

Building on the use cases and wireframes, we develop the project in a one-week cycle. The end goal of this is an MVP or prototype. This is typically for the duration of three months.

4

Continous Development

We continue to improve the MVP by adding all the desired features and employing continuous delivery/deployment technologies to seamlessly deploy changes without downtime.

5

Handoff

This concludes our engagement. You will have a fully-featured, clearly documented, and well-tested software application, tailor-made to give your business a competitive advantage as you create more customers.
